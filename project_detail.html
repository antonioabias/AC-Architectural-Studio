Hillside Modern Residence

A contemporary luxury home that seamlessly integrates with the natural hillside landscape, featuring sustainable materials and innovative design solutions that enhance both indoor and outdoor living experiences.

Design Story

When the Johnson family approached us with their vision for a modern family home that would honor the natural beauty of their hillside property, we knew this project would require a delicate balance between bold architectural statements and environmental sensitivity.

The site presented both opportunities and challenges—stunning panoramic views of the valley below, but steep terrain that required innovative structural solutions. Our design response was to create a series of terraced levels that follow the natural contours of the land, minimizing site disturbance while maximizing the connection between interior and exterior spaces.

The result is a 4,200 square foot residence that feels both grounded in its landscape and elevated above it, with floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the views like living artwork and outdoor terraces that extend the living spaces into the natural environment.
